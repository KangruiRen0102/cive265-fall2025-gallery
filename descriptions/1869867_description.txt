The reason behind my design is to create an area where students can gather and communicate with each other as fellow students. The experience I want pedestrians and new students to have is to feel the identity of being a panda/bear at the University of Alberta, hence being a den. Even though my design is open ended, my goal is that people enjoy walking through the den and also find it as a cozy space to socialize. During the week of welcome, I believe my design has the potential to bring new or current students together by being a topic of discussion, a place to rest and chat, or a place where students can remember that they are now part of the University of Alberta community once they walk through the den with full view of the main quad.
