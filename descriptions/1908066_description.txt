This magnificent pavilion will be a true delight to everyone who passes by. Its open and circular design will draw people in from all directions, to sit on the inner bench for a moment. It will bring people together with its smooth and user-friendly design, and has the potential to create lifelong friendships. This is a vital part of the Week of Welcome, as it will allow people to take a break during a long day of walking, and connect with the people around them in an open, stress-free pavilion. 

The design has four adjustable parameters; radius, height, round bar quantity, and square bar quantity. The radius is adjustable to allow for preferred size on the designated area. the height can be adjusted based on people's height, so that no one will hit their head on the bars. The quantity of round perimeter bars can be changed to create a more open or closed concept, and the quantity of square bars for seating cand be adjusted to optimize comport and visual appeal.
