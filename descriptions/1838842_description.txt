My design rationale is usability. The area of the quad this will be placed in will be off a 
high traffic pedway and usually is used for WOW and various clubs throughout the 
year. A structure that can protect against the elements, have structural integrity to 
support posters or fixtures for a extended period is perfect for the usage of the area. 
The pavilion should be used by pedestrians as shelter while walking to have a 
conversation, take pictures or just enjoy nature in a sheltered environment. The Week 
of Welcome events can utilize the pavilion to create events for the week and the 
versatility f the design allows for wide scale uses.
