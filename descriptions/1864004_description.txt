1. The white steel shell of my pavilion is meant to represent an egg shell with the top off while the brown wood spiraling cylinders are meant to represent a nest. Pedestrians will have the feeling of being in an egg and a bit of shade from the sun. For Week of Welcome, this pavilion is a metaphor for first years taking their first step into their future and new-found responsibilities; the top is off to signify maturity and independence.

2.Parameters allow you to allocate towards aesthetic or comfort; you can make the wood cylinders thinner to give the egg a larger radius and height, at the cost of the nest aesthetic. Parameters also allow you to change the size of the openings for ease of access
